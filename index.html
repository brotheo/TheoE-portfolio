about me

My name is Theophilus Ayepeh.

Originally from Ghana but studied outside my country after high school, I'm now living and working in Bangkok, Thailand. My education includes a bachelor's degree in information technology and a master's degree in applied information and communication technologies. During my undergraduate research, I focused on developing an eHealth management system, and my master's thesis centered on a Semantic Tourism Guide System using Ontology. Currently, I'm a computer science teacher at a highly regarded high school in Thailand. I enjoy introducing my students to a range of computer languages, including Python, HTML, CSS, and C++, as well as data science concepts. In addition to teaching technical skills, I'm passionate about incorporating project-based learning into my curriculum, which provides my students with practical opportunities to apply their knowledge and develop real-world skills. Given my background in eHealth and tourism technology, I'm eager to explore opportunities to apply my expertise to support innovative solutions in these areas. I'm also committed to staying up-to-date with the latest developments and technologies in the field by attending conferences, reading academic journals, and participating in online communities.
